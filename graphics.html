Evaluation of Web Graphics

Site Evaluated: Apple

Purpose of graphics on this site

The purpose of the graphics on this site is attract the audience's attention and keep the person on the page. It is also used to encourage the user to buy the product. The images on the front page of the site, including the banner at the top shows eye-catching images of the product to encourage people to purchase it. The biggest and most eye-catching pictures are near the top of the page.

Guidelines for effectuve use of graphics

  1. Yes - Graphics should fit in with the purpose, organization, and style of the page.
  2. Yes - Avoided using graphics with large file sizes that add to the load time of the page.
  3. Yes - Graphics helped to guide the viewers’ focus to the important content on the page.
  4. Yes - Avoided repetitive use of overly bright or potentially “obnoxious” images.
  5. Yes - Avoided the use of graphics to convey textual content information.
  6. Yes - Provide textual equivalent alternatives for graphic content.
  7. Yes - When using text in graphics, make sure there is sufficient contrast between the text and the background color.
